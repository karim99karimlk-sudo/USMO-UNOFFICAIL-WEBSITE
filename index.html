USMO History

Union Sportive Musulmane Oujda also known by USMO, One of the most historic Moroccan clubs .Founded in 1958 in the heart of Oujda, Morocco. The club's primary colours are black and white , The fans nicknamed the team by "The Stork of the East". Currently USMO is playing in the second division BOTOLA PRO 2.

The story began when the three friends: AbdelRahman Belaouchi, Benyounes Mezouari and Boumadien Betmouchent, came up with an idea of establishing a club after seeing the vibrant energy of youth in their neighborhood "CASABLANCA ROAD". The club's headquarters that time was in the Oulad Mimoun Cafe.

At first, they made Hajj Mimoun Moumen an honorary president after convincing him, Mekki Bouchaib as a talent scout , Hajj Elaarbi Ben Lakhder Darfoufi nicknamed by "Ba Aroub" as a coach , He is considered the first coach for Union .
